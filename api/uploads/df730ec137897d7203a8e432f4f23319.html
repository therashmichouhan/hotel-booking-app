Luxurious residence in the historic 16th-century Tower on the beach in Positano for a wonderful holiday full of unforgettable charm. Independent castle overlooking the sea, can accommodate up to 6 people.

The space
Ideal for a family or group of 6 people who want to live Positano day and night, visit the coast by sea in total relaxation. The tower is 300 metres from the beach, pier, shopping centre, restaurant, supermarket and take away! Walk away from traffic and chaos.
The accommodation is wonderful! Three bedrooms, three bathrooms with chromatic welness shower. A large living room welcomes guests in the magic of a circular space that traces the perimeter of the Tower. The balcony with a bifurcate, overlooking the sea, brings the horizon closer to those who look out over it. Furnished with antique furniture, piano, sofa, dining table, desk, armchairs, TV. The kitchen is equipped with refrigerator, microwave, American coffee maker, toaster, robot, centrifuge, ice maker, etc.
A romantic wrought-iron staircase allows access to the large circular terrace overlooking the sea of Positano. Paved with vintage overseas blue tiles depicting the rose of the winds, the terrace is furnished with comfortable deckchairs, wrought-iron table and chairs, typical of the area, and a comfortable canopy that captures the shade in the hottest hours. The circular terrace is ideal for celebrating intimate and extremely romantic symbolic weddings.
Hairdryer, washing machine, clothes dryer, ironing board and iron are available in the house.

Guest access
Guests are free to live the itera house.

Other things to note
The circular terrace overlooking the Positano Sea is ideal for celebrating intimate and extremely romantic symbolic weddings.
The accommodation is right on the cliff so the voice of the sea will undoubtedly keep our guests company.
For our guests we apply discounts of 5% to the restaurant "Capricci" which is also take away; discount of 10/15% in the Galleria "Art&Craft" where individual or group painting lessons are organized; of 15% in "Boutique Anna Passerotti" where every dress is (website hidden) in Positano and painted by hand.
